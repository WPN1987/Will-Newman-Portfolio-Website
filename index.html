About Me

At the start of 2019, I decided to make a career change away from retail after six years working night shifts. I wanted to challenge myself and learn new skills everyday, so I embarked on a journey to learn code via the Code Nation Master Course. I aspire to become a full-stack developer in the future through learning everyday and working on different projects. The reason I decided to enroll onto the Master Course at Code Nation was to learn how to code in an environment where I had support and advice, to help guide me through the learning process. The intensive nature of the course demands a steep learning curve, which I believe has helped improve me as a junior developer and I feel this has justified my decision to take the course as a route into the software development industry.

During the Master Course at Code Nation I have gained skills in, Javascript, HTML, CSS and learning how to problem solve using charts, diagrams and psuedo code. I have also learnt skills in Node.js, introduction to testing using jest, React, using the terminal and command line, using git and git hub. I am looking to use the skills I have learnt to help develop my career by earning an apprenticeship so I can return to Code Nation and improve my knowledge and build my web tool box further.

I have also attended Aberystwyth University, where I graduated with a Business and Management degree BSc (Econ) in 2011 and a Management and Marketing MSc (Econ) degree in 2013.

My main interest is spending time with family, friends and going for walks with my partner. A big interest of mine is football, and I have spent the majority of my life playing and watching football, I support Manchester United FC and England. Although I have not played football in a while, I found playing a great way to stay fit and meet new people. I tend to spend more time enjoying the tactical analysis of football. I also lke watching films and series, such as the Marvel Cinematic Universe films, and I am massive fan of Game of Thrones. Other interests include gaming on my PS4 and listening to music.
